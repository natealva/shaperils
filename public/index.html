? About Shayprils

Shayprils is a small, private check-in app for a group of ~20 friends from Harvard Business School meeting up at Shays Pub & Wine Bar in Cambridge, MA every weekday in April 2026.

Members sign in with their name and phone number, take a selfie when they arrive at Shays, and can optionally receive text message alerts when friends check in. The app runs for the month of April 2026 only (April 1 – April 30).

This is a non-commercial, invite-only project with no advertising, no marketing messages, and no data sharing with third parties.

✉ SMS Text Message Program
What messages you'll receive

If you choose to subscribe, you will receive SMS notifications when a fellow Shayprils member checks in at Shays bar. These are purely informational alerts — no marketing, no promotions, no ads.

Example: "Shayprils: Nate just checked in at Shays. Come join! Reply STOP to unsubscribe."
How you opt in (consent flow)

SMS is completely optional. After creating your account, you will see a "Notifications" toggle switch on the home screen. It is off by default. You must voluntarily tap this toggle to subscribe to text alerts. The app works fully without SMS — you are never required to opt in.

SMS consent is a standalone action, separate from account creation and separate from our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Creating an account does not enroll you in SMS messages.

How to opt out

You can unsubscribe at any time by replying STOP to any message, or by tapping the "Unsubscribe" button inside the app. Reply HELP for help. We honor all opt-out requests immediately.

Message frequency & rates

Typically 1–5 messages per day, only on weekdays in April 2026. Message and data rates may apply. Carrier surcharges may apply.

By subscribing to SMS alerts, you consent only to receiving Shayprils check-in notifications as described above. This consent is not bundled with, or a condition of, any other service, purchase, or agreement.
